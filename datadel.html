Facebook Data Deletion

MINIGAME LIMITED does not save your personal data on our servers.

According to the Facebook Apps and Websites Platform rules, we are required to provide a User Data Deletion Instructions URL. If you wish to delete your activities related to any Mini Mammoth Games products, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click on "Settings".
  3. Look for "Apps and Websites" where you will find all the apps and websites linked to your Facebook account.
  4. Find the option for the game you would like to remove data from and click "Remove".
  5. Confirm by clicking "Remove" on the pop-up that appears.

Congratulations, you have successfully removed your app activities.
